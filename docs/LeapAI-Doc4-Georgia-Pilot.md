## 90-Day Pilot Plan

### Phase 1 — Build (Weeks 1–6)

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1–2 | Fork OpenMAIC → rebrand LeapAI. Deploy to Vercel. |
| 3–4 | Load 20 prebuilt lessons. Test all lesson prompts. |
| 5–6 | Logto age-assurance + parental dashboard. Umami analytics. Formbricks consent forms. |

### Phase 2 — Partner (Weeks 7–8)

- Sign MOUs with 3 Georgia public libraries
- Train librarians as LeapAI Guides (2-hour session)
- Enroll employer cohort (Goodwill of North Georgia + 2 employers)
- Brief Georgia DOL on pilot metrics framework

### Phase 3 — Launch Cohorts (Weeks 9–10)

| Cohort | Age Band | Location | Size |
|--------|----------|----------|------|
| K–12 Session | Ages 9–13 | DeKalb County Library | 20–30 |
| Workforce Session | Ages 30–55 | Goodwill of North Georgia | 20–30 |
| Senior Session | Ages 65+ | Atlanta-Fulton Library | 15–20 |

### Phase 4 — Measure & Scale (Weeks 11–12)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Lesson completion rate | ≥ 75% |
| Quiz pass rate (70%+ score) | ≥ 60% of learners |
| NPS | ≥ 50 |
| Learners earning first micro-credential | 100% of completers |

**Deliverables:** Pilot outcomes report, Georgia LLA policy brief, GA Board of Education deck, press release.

## Georgia LLA Policy Brief Outline

**Title:** Closing Georgia's AI Readiness Gap: A Proposal for Georgia Lifelong Learning Accounts

1. **The Problem:** 84% of GA workers have zero AI training; top industries are disruption-vulnerable
2. **The Proposal:** $5,000 portable LLA per displaced worker, ages 16–99, administered through GA DOL
3. **The Platform:** LeapAI — open-source, free to deploy statewide, 20 prebuilt lessons
4. **The Ask:** $500K FY2027 appropriation + GA DOL pilot designation + Board of Ed K–12 alignment
