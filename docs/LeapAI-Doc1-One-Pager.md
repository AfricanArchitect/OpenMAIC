## What Is LeapAI?

LeapAI is the United States' first national lifelong AI learning and workforce transition platform —
free, open-source, and built for every American from age 5 to 120.

Built on the open-source **OpenMAIC** classroom engine (Tsinghua University / JCST 2026),
LeapAI turns any topic into a fully interactive AI classroom in one click — complete with
AI teachers, AI classmates, quizzes, simulations, whiteboard, and voice.

## The Solution: Three Interdependent Pillars

**Pillar A — LeapAI Platform (the learning engine)**
OpenMAIC-based interactive AI classroom. Any topic → full lesson in one click.
Prebuilt curriculum library: 4 national pillars × 5 age bands = 20 ready-to-launch lessons.
Self-hostable by schools, libraries, employers, and states.

**Pillar B — LeapAI Learning Passport (the credential system)**
Portable digital credential wallet. Stackable micro-credentials earned in weeks, not years.
Industry-verified by employer consortia. Federal aid eligible.
The U.S. answer to China's Credit Bank system.

**Pillar C — Lifelong Learning Accounts / LLAs (the funding mechanism)**
$5,000–$10,000 seed per worker, ages 16–99.
Replenished by employer contributions, individual savings (tax-incentivized), and federal/state grants.
Used on LeapAI or any accredited provider. Pilot: state consortia → national scale.
